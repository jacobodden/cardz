# Game
Pay Me

## Description
Pay Me is a dynamic, 11-round Contract Rummy game for 2 to 6 players. Players
build melds to empty their hands. Hand sizes increase by one card each round
(from 3 to 13 cards), and the wild card changes every round to match the number
of cards in play.

## Setup

### Deck
Use one standard deck for 2 players, two decks for 3-4, or three decks for
5-6. Jokers are ommitted.

### Setup
Place the remaining deck in the center as a draw pile and flip the top card face
up to start the discard pile.

### Wilds
All cards corresponding to the round number are wild (e.g., in Round 5, all
fives are wild).

## Gameplay Dealing 
The first hand deals 3 cards to each player. Each subsequent round adds 1 more
card, peaking at 13 cards in Round 11.

### Turns
On your turn, either draw the top face-up card from the discard pile, or draw
the top card from the stock pile.

### Melding
If you have valid melds (a group of 3+ of a kind, or 3+ consecutive cards in the
same suit), you can lay them on the table.

### Discarding
End your turn by discarding one card face up.

## Scoring
When you are able to lay down your entire hand (going out), you can say "Pay
Me. All other players may draw one more card to attempt to fininsh melding
theird hands. You then lay down your melded hand. Other players may play off of
your melded hand using any un-melded cards in their own hand.

i.e.
- adding another 4 to your set of fours.
- adding a 9 of clubs to your run of 5,6,7,8 of clubs (must match suit)

- Face cards (Jacks, Queens, Kings) are worth 10.
- Aces are worth 1.
- Numbered cards are worth their face value.

The player with the lowest score at the end of all 11 rounds wins the pot.
